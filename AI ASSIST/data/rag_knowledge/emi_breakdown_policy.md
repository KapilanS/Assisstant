# EMI Breakdown and Schedule Policy (Structured Knowledge - RAG)

## EMI Components

### Principal Component
- Reduces outstanding loan balance
- Lower in initial EMIs, increases over tenure
- Total principal over tenure equals loan amount

### Interest Component
- Calculated on reducing balance
- Higher in initial EMIs, decreases over tenure
- Depends on interest rate and outstanding principal

## EMI Schedule
- Provided at loan disbursement
- Available in app and net banking under Loan Details
- Shows: EMI number, date, principal, interest, balance, cumulative interest

## Prepayment
- Part prepayment: Reduces principal; options to reduce EMI or tenure
- Full prepayment: Closes loan; NOC issued after clearance
- Prepayment charges: May apply for fixed rate or within lock-in period (product-specific)
- No prepayment charges on most floating rate retail loans (as per regulatory norms)

## Restructuring
- Available for eligible customers facing financial hardship
- Options: Tenure extension, EMI reduction, moratorium
- Interest continues to accrue during moratorium
- Eligibility assessed on case-by-case basis
