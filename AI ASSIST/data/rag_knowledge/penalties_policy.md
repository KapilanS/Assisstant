# Penalties and Charges Policy (Structured Knowledge - RAG)

## Late Payment Charges
- **Loan EMI:** Typically 2-3% of overdue amount per month (capped as per product)
- **Credit Card:** Minimum due late fee as per card terms
- **Grace Period:** 3 days from due date for loans (product-specific)

## Bounce Charges
- **Cheque/ECS Bounce:** As per tariff (typically Rs. 200-500 per bounce)
- **Penalty for repeated bounces:** May lead to account restriction
- **Impact:** Reported to credit bureau; affects credit score

## Foreclosure Charges
- **Home Loans (Floating):** Generally nil for foreclosure
- **Home Loans (Fixed):** May attract 2-3% of principal as foreclosure charges
- **Personal Loans:** Varies by product; some have nil charges
- **Lock-in Period:** Foreclosure during lock-in may attract additional charges

## Processing Fees
- Non-refundable once processing has commenced
- Waived or discounted under promotional schemes
- GST applicable on processing fees

## Duplicate Document Charges
- Nominal fee for duplicate statements, NOC, interest certificate
- Waived in certain circumstances (e.g., first request)

## Minimum Balance Charges
- Applicable if average balance falls below required minimum
- Varies by account type (typically Rs. 200-500 per instance)
- Zero balance accounts exempt
