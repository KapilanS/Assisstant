# Policy Rules and Compliance (Structured Knowledge - RAG)

## KYC Compliance
- Valid KYC required for all accounts and transactions
- Periodic KYC updation as per regulatory norms
- Video KYC and e-KYC accepted where permitted

## Loan Disbursement
- Disbursement only after documentation and verification complete
- For home loans: Disbursement linked to construction/property milestones where applicable
- Maximum disbursement: As per LTV and valuation

## Credit Assessment
- Based on credit score, income, existing obligations, employment
- No discrimination on prohibited grounds
- Transparent communication of eligibility and rejection reasons (through grievance channel)

## Grievance Redressal
- Level 1: Branch/Customer Care
- Level 2: Grievance Officer (response within 30 days)
- Level 3: Banking Ombudsman (if unresolved)

## Data Protection
- Customer data handled as per applicable data protection regulations
- No sharing of sensitive information without consent
- Secure channels for transmission

## Regulatory Compliance
- All operations aligned with RBI/IRDAI and other applicable regulations
- Rates and terms subject to regulatory changes
- Customer communication in compliance with advertising guidelines
